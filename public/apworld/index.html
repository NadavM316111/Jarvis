Key Concepts You MUST Know

Document Analysis (DBQ)

  • HIPP: Historical context, Intended audience, Purpose, Point of view
  • Sourcing - who wrote it and why
  • Corroboration - compare multiple docs
  • Outside evidence - add what you know

Comparison Essays (LEQ)

  • Direct comparisons with evidence
  • Explain reasons for similarities/differences
  • Make connections across time periods
  • Strong thesis with argument

Causation Questions

  • Short-term vs long-term causes
  • Direct vs indirect effects
  • Turning points in history
  • Unintended consequences
