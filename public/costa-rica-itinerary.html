Pro Tips

Pack Light & Smart

Bring quick-dry clothes, water shoes for the waterfall, bug spray with DEET, and reef-safe sunscreen. It's rainy season - pack a light rain jacket!

Cash is King

Many local spots prefer colones (Costa Rican currency), but USD is widely accepted. ATMs are available in La Fortuna town.

Book Activities in Advance

Memorial Day weekend is busy! Book ziplines and tours at least a week ahead. The safari float often sells out.

Download Offline Maps

Cell service can be spotty in the jungle. Download Google Maps offline for the La Fortuna area before you leave.

Embrace "Tico Time"

Things move slower here - that's the charm! Don't over-schedule. Leave time for spontaneous adventures and relaxation.

Wildlife Tip

Best wildlife viewing is early morning (6-8am) and dusk. Look UP - sloths, monkeys, and toucans are in the trees!
