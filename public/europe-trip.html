Flight to Paris

Paris, France

3 Days

Experience the magic of the City of Light. From the iconic Eiffel Tower to the charming streets of Montmartre, Paris awaits with its timeless elegance, world-class cuisine, and artistic treasures.

10:00 AM

Arrive at Charles de Gaulle Airport

Clear customs, grab luggage, take taxi or RER B train to city center

12:30 PM

Hotel Check-in & Freshen Up

Drop bags, quick shower, get ready for exploration

2:00 PM

Lunch at a Local Bistro

Classic French onion soup, croissants, cafe au lait

4:00 PM

Eiffel Tower Visit

Take the elevator to the summit for panoramic views of Paris

7:00 PM

Seine River Cruise

Evening dinner cruise watching the city lights twinkle

10:00 PM

Champs-Elysees Night Walk

Stroll down the famous avenue, see the Arc de Triomphe lit up

8:30 AM

Breakfast at Cafe de Flore

Historic cafe in Saint-Germain-des-Pres, famous for intellectuals

10:00 AM

The Louvre Museum

See the Mona Lisa, Venus de Milo, and thousands of masterpieces

1:30 PM

Lunch in Tuileries Garden

Grab a crepe and relax in the beautiful royal garden

3:00 PM

Notre-Dame Cathedral Area

Walk around the historic cathedral (exterior), explore Ile de la Cite

5:30 PM

Latin Quarter Exploration

Wander narrow streets, browse bookshops at Shakespeare & Company

8:00 PM

Dinner in Le Marais

Trendy neighborhood with amazing restaurants and nightlife

9:00 AM

Montmartre Morning

Climb to Sacre-Coeur Basilica, enjoy views over Paris

11:00 AM

Place du Tertre

Watch artists paint, maybe get a portrait done

12:30 PM

Final French Lunch

Coq au vin, escargot, finish with creme brulee

2:30 PM

Last-minute Shopping

Pick up macarons from Laduree, souvenirs

4:30 PM

Head to Airport

Au revoir Paris! Next stop: Rome

Flight to Rome

Rome, Italy

2 Days

Step into the Eternal City where ancient history meets vibrant modern life. Walk in the footsteps of emperors at the Colosseum, toss a coin in the Trevi Fountain, and indulge in authentic Italian cuisine.

9:00 AM

Arrive in Rome

Land at Fiumicino, train to Termini Station, check into hotel

11:00 AM

Colosseum Tour

Skip-the-line guided tour of the iconic amphitheater

1:30 PM

Roman Forum & Palatine Hill

Wander through ancient ruins where Roman life happened

3:30 PM

Lunch near Piazza Venezia

Authentic cacio e pepe, supplì, cold Peroni

5:00 PM

Trevi Fountain

Toss a coin to ensure your return to Rome!

6:30 PM

Spanish Steps & Shopping

Sit on the famous steps, browse Via Condotti boutiques

8:30 PM

Dinner in Trastevere

Charming neighborhood, amazing trattorias, live music

7:30 AM

Early Vatican Museums

Beat the crowds! See the Sistine Chapel ceiling in peace

10:30 AM

St. Peter's Basilica

Marvel at Michelangelo's Pieta, climb the dome for views

1:00 PM

Lunch at Pizzarium

Best pizza al taglio in Rome - Gabriele Bonci's famous spot

3:00 PM

Pantheon Visit

2000-year-old temple with the famous oculus dome

4:30 PM

Gelato at Giolitti

Historic gelateria since 1900 - try pistachio and stracciatella

6:00 PM

Piazza Navona Evening

Relax by Bernini's fountains, watch street performers

8:30 PM

Final Roman Feast

Carbonara, amatriciana, tiramisu - eat like a Roman!

4-Hour Bullet Train to Venice

Venice, Italy

3 Days

Glide through the enchanting canals of La Serenissima. This floating city of bridges and gondolas offers unmatched romance, stunning architecture, and a pace of life that's been unchanged for centuries.

9:00 AM

High-Speed Train from Rome

Board the Frecciarossa, enjoy Italian countryside views

1:00 PM

Arrive at Santa Lucia Station

Step out to your first view of the Grand Canal - magical!

2:00 PM

Water Taxi to Hotel

Navigate the canals to your accommodation

3:30 PM

Rialto Bridge & Market

Cross the iconic bridge, explore the fish market area

5:30 PM

Aperitivo Time

Spritz and cicchetti at a canal-side bar

7:30 PM

Sunset Gondola Ride

Classic Venetian experience through quiet back canals

9:00 PM

Dinner with Canal View

Fresh seafood risotto, sarde in saor, prosecco

8:00 AM

St. Mark's Square (Early)

See the piazza empty before tourist crowds arrive

9:00 AM

St. Mark's Basilica

Golden mosaics, Byzantine architecture, incredible interior

10:30 AM

Campanile Bell Tower

Elevator to the top for panoramic lagoon views

12:00 PM

Vaporetto to Murano

Famous glass-making island, watch artisans blow glass

2:30 PM

Burano Island

Rainbow-colored houses, lace-making tradition, amazing photos

5:00 PM

Return to Venice

Leisurely boat ride back through the lagoon

7:30 PM

Dinner in Dorsoduro

Local neighborhood away from tourists, authentic cuisine

9:00 AM

Doge's Palace Tour

Gothic masterpiece, Bridge of Sighs, secret passages

11:30 AM

Get Lost on Purpose

Wander the maze of alleys, discover hidden squares

1:00 PM

Final Italian Lunch

Bigoli in salsa, fegato alla veneziana, tiramisu

3:00 PM

Last Minute Shopping

Venetian masks, Murano glass jewelry, souvenirs

5:00 PM

Head to Marco Polo Airport

Water bus or taxi through the lagoon - final views
