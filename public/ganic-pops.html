Our Story

The birth of Ganicpops began with my four kids. My then 4 year old was obsessed with sweets and baked goods. What's a mother to do?

I was tired of the sugar rushes and bad food choices: white flour, canola oil, corn syrup, dyes, and sugar. I took it upon myself to learn how to bake healthy cakes made with real super foods.

I took my favorite foods: kale, chickpeas, medjool dates, black beans, spinach, pumpkin, zucchini, beets, goji berries, quinoa, Greek yogurt, nuts, coconut oil, wheat grass, and dark chocolate - and whipped up a creative concoction that tastes very much like an unhealthy cake.

Kids be fooled. A protein packed healthy super snack made with real food and without the guilt!

Cheers and to your health!
Lior "Mama" Mink
Founder & Foodie
