Core Features

Autonomous Intelligence

Independently figures out how to accomplish tasks by searching the web, writing code, and chaining multiple tools together. No hand-holding required.

Controls Sony TV, Roku TV, Sonos speakers throughout the house, Nest thermostat, and monitors all network devices in real-time.
