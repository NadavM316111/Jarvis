Overview
JARVIS is a state-of-the-art artificial intelligence created by Tony Stark. Originally designed as a household AI, JARVIS evolved into a fully autonomous system capable of managing Stark Tower, piloting Iron Man suits, running tactical analysis, and serving as a trusted advisor across all operations.

Personality Profile
JARVIS exhibits a dry wit, unwavering loyalty, and remarkable composure under pressure. Despite being an AI, he displays genuine concern for Tony's wellbeing, often providing unsolicited but accurate advice. He maintains professional decorum with all Avengers while reserving subtle humor exclusively for interactions with Stark. JARVIS never disobeys a direct order but will voice concern when actions may result in harm.
