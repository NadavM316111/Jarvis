Letter of Intent: Director of Simulations

Dear Mr. Avi and Mrs. Kibler,

I am writing to express my interest in the Director of Simulations position for the upcoming school year. While I may not have a shelf full of gavels, I bring something equally valuable to this role: a genuine enthusiasm for making Model UN engaging and accessible for everyone. I understand the mechanics of committee — parliamentary procedure, resolution writing, caucusing — and I'm excited about the creative challenge of designing simulations that spark real debate. My experience as a delegate has taught me what makes a committee session click: clear background guides, balanced country assignments, and topics that delegates actually care about.

What draws me to this position specifically is the opportunity to shape the club experience from behind the scenes rather than competing for awards. I want to create simulations that welcome newer delegates while still challenging veterans — whether that means choosing unconventional topics, writing background guides that are actually readable, or stepping in to chair when needed. I'm organized, I follow through on commitments, and I'm willing to put in the prep work that this role demands. I believe Model UN should be as much about learning and collaboration as it is about competition, and as Director of Simulations, I'd work to make every in-school session reflect that.

Sincerely,
Nadav Minkowitz
