Background & Research

Deep dive into the problem space, market landscape, and competitive analysis that shaped our vision for Sesami.

The Problem We're Solving

Understanding the critical challenges parents face in monitoring infant development

The Silent Struggle

Over 15% of infants experience developmental delays, yet most aren't identified until after their second birthday. This critical window of early intervention is often missed because parents lack the tools and knowledge to recognize early warning signs.

"I wish I had known sooner. The first two years matter so much, and I felt completely in the dark."

- Parent of a child with developmental delay

Market Analysis

A rapidly growing market with significant opportunity for innovation

Global Market Size

The global baby monitoring market is projected to reach $47.8 billion by 2030, growing at a CAGR of 8.2%. Smart baby monitors represent the fastest-growing segment.

Target Demographic

Millennial and Gen-Z parents, who represent 80% of new parents, actively seek technology-driven solutions for childcare and are willing to invest in premium products.

Market Gap

Current products focus on safety monitoring. Zero mainstream solutions address developmental tracking with AI-powered insights - a massive untapped opportunity.

"Parents today are more informed and proactive than ever. They want data, insights, and actionable guidance - not just alerts."

- Dr. Sarah Chen, Pediatric Development Specialist
