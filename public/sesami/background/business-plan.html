Business Plan

Strategic roadmap for bringing Sesami to market

Executive Summary

Sesami is an AI-powered infant developmental monitoring system that fills a critical gap in the baby monitor market. By combining computer vision technology with developmental science, Sesami enables parents to track their baby's motor milestones automatically and receive early alerts about potential developmental delays.

$520M
estimated initial target market opportunity

Mission & Vision

Mission Statement

To empower parents with AI-powered insights into their child's developmental journey, enabling earlier detection of delays and better outcomes through proactive monitoring.

Vision Statement

A world where every child receives the early intervention they need because developmental concerns are identified in weeks, not years.

Market Opportunity

Total Addressable Market (TAM)

The global smart baby monitor market is projected to reach $3.5 billion by 2034, growing at 8.2% CAGR. The US market specifically is expected to reach $740 million by 2033.

Target Customer Segments

  • Primary: Tech-forward millennial/Gen-Z parents (42% of new parents)
  • Secondary: Parents with family history of developmental concerns
  • Tertiary: Pediatric practices seeking patient monitoring tools

Market Positioning

Sesami positions itself in the premium smart monitor segment ($300-450) with unique differentiation through developmental tracking - a feature no competitor currently offers.

Product Strategy

Core Product

Sesami Camera System ($349): HD camera with on-device AI processing, companion mobile app, and cloud-based developmental analytics.

Product Add-ons

  • Sesami Wear ($79): Optional IMU sensor for wearable tracking
  • Sesami Pro subscription ($9.99/month): Advanced analytics, milestone predictions, pediatrician reports

Product Roadmap

Phase 1 - Q4 2026

MVP Launch

Core camera system with 5 motor milestone detections, basic sleep tracking, mobile app

Phase 2 - Q2 2027

Feature Expansion

12 milestone detections, wearable accessory launch, pediatrician report feature

Phase 3 - Q4 2027

Platform Growth

Social-emotional tracking, fine motor detection, telehealth integrations

Phase 4 - 2028

Ecosystem

Third-party integrations, international expansion, B2B pediatric partnerships

Go-to-Market Strategy

Launch Strategy

  1. Pre-launch (3 months before): Build waitlist via targeted social media ads, parenting influencer partnerships
  2. Launch: Direct-to-consumer via sesami.com, Amazon listing
  3. Growth (6 months post-launch): Expand to Buy Buy Baby, Target baby section
  4. Scale (Year 2): Pediatric office partnerships, B2B licensing

Competitive Advantages

First Mover Advantage

Sesami is the first consumer product to offer AI-powered developmental milestone tracking. This creates a 12-18 month head start before competitors can replicate.

  • Technology moat: Proprietary ML models trained on infant movement data
  • Data advantage: Longitudinal developmental data improves models over time
  • Clinical validation: Expert-backed approach builds trust
  • Network effects: More users = better benchmark comparisons for all

Team Requirements

Founding Team Needs

  • CEO: Experience in consumer hardware, parenting industry knowledge
  • CTO: Computer vision/ML expertise, edge computing experience
  • Chief Medical Advisor: Developmental pediatrics credentials, AAP connections
  • VP Product: Consumer app experience, user research background

Initial Team (Launch)

8-12 FTEs: Engineering (5), Product (2), Marketing (2), Operations (1), Customer Success (2)
