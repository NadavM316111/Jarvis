Unit Economics

Key Assumptions

  • Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC): $85 initially, dropping to $55 at scale
  • Subscription conversion rate: 40% of hardware purchasers
  • Average subscription length: 14 months
  • Hardware COGS: $157 (45% of retail price)

With a blended LTV:CAC ratio exceeding 3x by Year 2, Sesami demonstrates strong unit economics suitable for venture-scale growth. The subscription component significantly improves lifetime value over hardware-only models.
