Define Phase

Purpose for Innovation

Understanding why Sesami exists and the fundamental problem we are solving for families.

Why We Built Sesami

Every parent deserves peace of mind about their child's development. Sesami exists to bridge the gap between anxious uncertainty and informed confidence, providing continuous developmental insights that empower families and healthcare providers alike.

Our Vision

"To create a world where no developmental delay goes undetected, where every parent has access to objective data about their child's progress, and where early intervention becomes the norm rather than the exception."

The Problem We're Solving

Understanding the critical gaps in current developmental monitoring

1 in 6 Children have a developmental disability (CDC, 2023)
37% Are identified before kindergarten entry
2.7 yrs Average delay between first concern and diagnosis
$17B Annual cost of delayed intervention in the US

Time-Limited Checkups

Pediatricians have an average of 18 minutes per well-child visit to assess development, nutrition, behavior, and dozens of other health factors. Subtle delays are easily missed in these brief encounters.

Parental Recall Bias

Parents are asked to remember developmental milestones that happened weeks or months ago. Research shows parental recall has only 60-70% accuracy for motor milestones, leading to unreliable assessments.

Subjective Assessments

Current screening tools rely heavily on questionnaires and observational checklists. These subjective methods have sensitivity rates of only 70-80%, missing nearly a quarter of children who need intervention.

Parental Anxiety

78% of new parents report significant anxiety about their child's development. Without objective data, they turn to unreliable internet searches and comparison with other children, often increasing stress.

Who Benefits from Sesami

Our innovation serves multiple stakeholders in the child development ecosystem

Parents & Caregivers

  • Real-time insights into developmental progress
  • Reduced anxiety through objective data
  • Early alerts when intervention may be needed
  • Better preparation for pediatric visits
  • Peace of mind through continuous monitoring

Pediatricians

  • Longitudinal data between visits
  • Objective movement and milestone tracking
  • Better informed clinical decisions
  • Reduced reliance on parental recall
  • More efficient use of appointment time

Early Intervention Specialists

  • Earlier referrals for therapy services
  • Baseline data for treatment planning
  • Progress tracking during intervention
  • Evidence for insurance authorization
  • Improved outcomes through early start

Healthcare Systems

  • Reduced long-term intervention costs
  • Better population health data
  • Improved screening efficiency
  • Higher quality metrics for HEDIS measures
  • Value-based care alignment

Our Mission

To democratize developmental monitoring by providing every family with affordable, accurate, and actionable insights about their child's growth - transforming parental anxiety into informed confidence and ensuring no child falls through the cracks of the healthcare system.
