Key Insights

The most impactful discoveries from our empathize research

75% of parents worry about developmental milestones at least weekly

This anxiety is driven by a combination of information overload from the internet, social comparison at playgroups, and insufficient time during pediatric visits to address concerns thoroughly.

Parents have only 12-15 minutes of focused developmental observation time daily

Direct observation revealed that multitasking, work demands, and sibling care leave precious little time for parents to actively monitor their infant's development - making passive monitoring essential.

91% of parents require pediatrician endorsement before trusting AI insights

Trust in technology-driven developmental assessment is heavily dependent on medical professional validation, shaping our go-to-market strategy around clinical partnerships.
