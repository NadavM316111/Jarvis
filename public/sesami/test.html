Key Learnings

Insights that will shape our next iteration

What Worked Well

  • Passive monitoring - Parents loved not having to manually log anything
  • Milestone celebrations - Automated clips when milestones were detected
  • Pediatrician reports - Doctors appreciated having objective data

Areas for Improvement

  • Night vision quality - Detection accuracy dropped in low light
  • Alert frequency - Some parents wanted more control over notifications
  • Multi-child switching - UI needed improvement for parents of twins
