WHO WE ARE

BORN FROM THE STATIC

We're not your parents' rock band. Static Rebellion emerged from garage sessions, broken strings, and the raw frustration of teenage life. We channel the chaos, the confusion, and the unfiltered emotion into every riff, every scream, every beat.

Influenced by the Seattle sound, 90s grunge legends, and the modern alternative scene, we're carrying the torch for a new generation that refuses to be silenced.
